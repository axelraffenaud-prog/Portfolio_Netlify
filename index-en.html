Executive Profile

A hands-on executive, driven by performance

For over twenty years, I have supported organisations through their growth, restructuring and transformation phases. From commercial leadership to executive committee governance, I have built a career where every role was an opportunity to create measurable value: ARR multiplied by four, structured pipelines, professionalised sales forces, and economic models adapted in times of crisis.

My experience spans diverse environments — SaaS B2B, IT services, organisational consulting, industry and services — with one constant: the ability to align strategy, execution and the collective to deliver sustainable results. I have managed P&Ls of several million euros, coordinated networks of 70 consultants, and supervised up to 120 salespeople across multi-site operations.

Today, I am seeking an executive mandate — Deputy CEO, CRO or CEO — within an ambitious SME or mid-cap, with a particular appetite for transformation, build-up or equity participation projects. I bring proven operational experience, a solid network, and a demanding standard of results.
